What Is Family Tree Login?
--------------------------

A **family tree login** is the sign-in process used to access an online genealogy platform where your family history is stored. After logging in, you can:

* View your family tree.
* Add parents, children, and relatives.
* Upload family photos and historical documents.
* Search genealogy records.
* Collaborate with family members.
* Save and update your research.

How to Log In to Your Family Tree Account
-----------------------------------------

Follow these simple steps to access your account:

1. Open the official genealogy website.
2. Click the **Login** or **Sign In** button.
3. Enter your registered email address or username.
4. Type your password.
5. Click **Sign In** to access your dashboard.

Once logged in, you can continue researching and updating your family history.

Common Family Tree Login Problems
---------------------------------

Forgotten Password
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

If you forget your password:

* Select **Forgot Password**.
* Enter your registered email address.
* Follow the password reset instructions sent to your email.

Incorrect Login Information
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

If your login fails:

* Check your email address.
* Verify your password.
* Make sure Caps Lock is turned off.

Browser Problems
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

Sometimes your browser may prevent a successful login.

Try the following:

* Clear browser cache.
* Delete cookies.
* Update your browser.
* Try another browser or device.

Website Maintenance
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

Occasionally, genealogy websites undergo scheduled maintenance. If the website is temporarily unavailable, wait a few minutes before trying again.

How to Create a Family Tree Account
-----------------------------------

If you don't have an account, follow these steps:

1. Visit the genealogy website.
2. Click **Create Account**.
3. Enter your name.
4. Provide your email address.
5. Create a secure password.
6. Verify your email.
7. Begin building your family tree.
